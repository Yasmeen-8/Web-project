Resources and Information

Mental Health Resources

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline - A 24/7 helpline providing support for people in distress.
  • Crisis Text Line - Text "HELLO" to 741741 to connect with a trained crisis counselor.
  • Mental Health America - Resources and information on mental health.

Academic Support

  • Khan Academy - Free online courses, lessons, and practice.
  • Coursera - Access to online courses from universities.
  • Quizlet - Study tools and flashcards to help with learning.

Community Activities

  • Meetup - Find and join local groups and activities.
  • Eventbrite - Discover local events and community gatherings.
  • Campus Activities - Find events at colleges and universities.

Financial Resources

  • Fastweb - Scholarship search engine for financial aid opportunities.
  • NerdWallet - Tools for managing finances.
  • Federal Student Aid - Information on federal financial aid for students.
