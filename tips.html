For students:
Finding motivation to study shouldn’t be your focus, try disciplining yourself
into having a steady study schedule and time to make sure you’re up to date with
your subjects and lessons and avoid cramming before an exam. First know your style
are you the type to study before relaxing? For example, you must finish all your
homework before indulging in someone fun like movies or Video games and other
activities. Well, its recommended after your day in uni or during the weekends
an early study time before doing anything fun, but if you’re the opposite!
Try doing the fun stuff and relax in the morning or after uni and then at
night 2-4 hours before sleep pull out your iPad, laptop or books to study
and do your homework. A lot of people would say the second part is invalid
and can cause people to be lazy but know that you’re valid and as long as
you at least study you’re doing amazing no matter what time works for you.
The main tip! Remove your phone, no limits no nothing just put your phone away
from your desk. Have a certain space for studying! The desk you have your computer
on and play video games? No good you need a dedicated study area it helps your
brain get into the study zone. Practice taking short breaks between sessions,
like using the pomodoro timer if it works for you. Set small realistic Goals
like studying one or 2 lessons and one homework. Remember to work out and move!
Keep your body busy with an activity it will make you more energized to study
and help you focus, improve your discipline by setting a time for a 10 min
workout every day, it doesn’t have to be a lot and especially not reliant
on your motivation!

ADHD tips for studying:
Make sure to use organizational tools, such as notion, Todo lists, sticky notes
and marks into the pages. Practice discipline, try to set a time with no
distractions or anyone bothering you and study in the set time every day or so.
Make sure to take often breaks! Walk if you feel restless and restricted or stretch
often. It’s recommended to sit in front of the class which can improve your focus and
pressure push you to avoid distractions. Its important that you let your teachers and
administrators know about it, to discuss ways to support or extra time in exams and
similar benefits that can help you focus. Ask your teachers for visual aids like pictures
flowcharts and graphic organizers if not available.

For studying use planners and reminders, you can even ask someone to keep a watchful eye
over you to make sure you do the work. Create a steady schedule and try your best to follow
it, during studying maybe consider trying the pomodoro technique 25 mins of studying and
then 5 mins of break. You might lose track of time so make sure to have a watch or staying
aware of it with timers and clocks, Don’t worry about asking for support it would be a very
important step to improve your lifestyle and minimize struggles. Remember its important and
recommended to ask for help whenever you need, theres all kinds of support ready for any type
of Condition you could be suffering. Make sure you’re aware and equipped to support yourself
and others
